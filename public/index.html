Start Your Whale Learning Journey

Each section turns the school presentation into easy-to-follow learning moments. Dive in and explore the wonderful world of whales, one stop at a time.

Whale Introduction

Discover what makes whales so special. Learn why these gentle giants are mammals, not fish, and how they've adapted to life in the ocean.

Types of Whales

Meet the Blue Whale, Humpback Whale, and Orca. Compare their sizes, shapes, and unique features in a simple, visual guide.

What Whales Eat

From tiny krill to small fish, explore the diet of different whales. See how baleen whales filter-feed and toothed whales hunt.

How Whales Breathe

Whales breathe air just like us! Find out how blowholes work, why whales surface, and how they can hold their breath for so long underwater.

This content is beginner-friendly and designed especially for elementary learners.

Why This Project Matters

This site transforms a simple classroom assignment into a memorable learning experience that sparks curiosity in children, offers engaging resources for teachers, and invites supportive families to explore the wonders of the ocean together.

Created entirely by two 4th-grade students, this project celebrates curiosity, teamwork, and the joy of science learning. It proves that even the youngest explorers can build something that educates and inspires.

  • Student-Made Learning

    Built by kids for kids, making complex topics approachable and fun through a peer-to-peer perspective.

  • Easy Science for Kids

    Simplifying marine biology into bite-sized, colorful lessons that align with elementary education standards.

  • Visual Storytelling

    Using cartoon whales and vibrant illustrations to create a narrative that captures young imaginations.

Meet the Whale Stars

Each whale has a special story. Let's explore three amazing giants of the ocean together — compare their size, shape, and style to discover what makes every one of them a true sea champion.

Blue Whale

  • The biggest animal on Earth — even bigger than any dinosaur!
  • Its heart is as heavy as a small car — imagine that bump-bump sound underwater!

Humpback Whale

  • Famous for its beautiful songs that travel for miles through the sea.
  • It loves to leap and splash — the acrobat of the ocean!

Orca

  • The fastest swimmer in the whale family — built for speed and teamwork.
  • Its black-and-white suit makes it look like the ocean's superhero!
